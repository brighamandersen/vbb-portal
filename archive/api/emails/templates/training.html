Hello Mentor,

Your first session is your opportunity to get to know your student! Below are a few good questions to get things started:

  • Tell me about your family?
  • What do you like to study in school?
  • What do you like to do?

Tip: After each question the student answers, answer the question yourself. They are as interested to learn about you as you are about them!

Here is a link to a mentoring guide that you can reference as you create lesson plans. After the first session, take what your student would like to learn about, and build upon that each week as they progress. Mentoring Guide

Check out our mentor success training module at Mentor Training

It might take two or three sessions to get them to be open, be patient as your friendship with your student forms.
